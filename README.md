##  Bridge Between Investor & Business People**

```markdown
#  Bridge Between Investor & Business People

A web-based platform that connects business owners, investors, bankers, and advisors — enabling smooth communication and efficient collaboration.  
The system allows users to register under different roles and interact through proposals, funding requests, and advisory support.

---

##  Features by Role

###  Business Person
- Register / log in via Firebase Authentication
- Post business ideas and funding requirements
- View proposals from investors

###  Investor
- Register / log in as investor
- View business ideas and send investment proposals

###  Banker
- Add loan options and funding details for businesses

###  Business Advisor
- Share business improvement information
- View queries and post advisory solutions

###  User
- View all business categories

---

##  Tech Stack
| Component | Technology |
|------------|-------------|
| Frontend | HTML, CSS, JavaScript |
| Backend | Firebase Authentication, Firebase Firestore |
| Database | Firestore (NoSQL) |
| Hosting | GitHub Pages / Firebase Hosting |
